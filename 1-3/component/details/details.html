Menu (Kapao)

Ingredients

400 grams of pork
50 grams of basil leaves
2 cloves of garlic
12 red bird’s eye chilies
6 dried red bird’s eye chilies
1 tablespoon of fish sauce
1 1/2 tablespoons of green-label seasoning sauce
3 tablespoons of oyster sauce

Ingredients

Heat oil in a hot pan.
Stir-fry the chilies and garlic in the oil until they turn a light color.
Add the minced pork and continue to stir-fry until it is 75% cooked.
Add the seasonings and stir to combine.
Add the basil leaves at the end, mix well, and turn off the heat immediately.
Fry an egg sunny-side up to your desired level of doneness.
Serve the rice on a plate, top with the basil pork stir-fry, and finish with the fried egg.
